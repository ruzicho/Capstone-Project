Online Orientation

If you cannot attend a face-to-face Orientation session, please enroll in the BlackBoard Online Orientation.

Log in with your My Tir-c Username and Password. Click Login. At "Enroll yourself as a member of this course…", click "Yes".

You will practice posting to a discussion forum, submitting an assignment and taking an online quiz.


Orientation Dates

First time taking an online course?
If you are brand new to Online Learning at CCC, we strongly advise you to attend a face-to-face BlackBoard Orientation.

Do I have to do anything before I come to the Orientation?
You should login to your My Tri-c account before coming to the orientation. You may stop by the TLC on the main campus, TLC 111, about 30 minutes before the orientation if you need help logging in.

What if I'm going to the South County Center Orientation?
Come about 30 minutes before the orientation begins if you need help logging in to your My Tri-c account.
