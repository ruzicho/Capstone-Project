Student Tip

It is easy to fall behind when you don't have to show up for class. Instead of attending on-campus classes 3 times a week, plan to login to your online course at least 3 times a week. Creating this rountine will only help you succeed and get the most out of class.

Login Information


To Login:

  1. Open the Google Chrome browser.

  2. Go to http://www.tri-c.edu/

  3. On the Tri-c website, click the My Tri-c space logo

  4. Enter your Username.
    Username: s0000000 (replace zeros with your Student ID number)
    Your Student ID number can be found on your course schedule next to “Student ID:” For example, if your Student ID is listed on your schedule as 0012345, your Username would be s0012345.

  5. Enter your Password: *********
    For new students, your initial password is the last four digits of your Social Security number.
    You will be prompted to change your password to one of your own choosing.
    New passwords must be a minimum of 8 characters in length and contain letters and numbers.
    Note: your password expires every 6 months. My Tri-c space will prompt you to change it.
    If you are a returning student, reset your My Tri-c password at the beginning of each semester.
    Go to http://www.tri-c.edu/

    Follow the instructions at the bottom of the screne. How can I reset my My Tri-c password?’.

  6. Click Log On.
    Note that both the username and password are case sensitive.

  7. Under the “My Courses in BlackBoard” click on the course name to access the course
    (Courses will be available on the first day of classes).
    If your “My Courses in BlackBoard” don’t display, click on the Refresh button to the right.

  8. The first time you access BlackBoard from My Tri-c space, you may get the following message:
    'The login to BlackBoard failed.
    SSO credentials for user "0#.w|Tri-c\s0000000" could not be found in application "BlackBoardSSO"
    Enter your credentials.'
    Click Enter your credentials. Enter your My Tri-c username and password.
    You will not need to ‘Enter your credentials’ again until you change your password.

  9. Once you have entered your username and password you will be taken to your BlackBoard course or courses.
