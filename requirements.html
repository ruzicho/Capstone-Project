Technical Requirements


Computer Requirements

For PC computers:

  • Windows 7 Operating System (or better)

For Macintosh computers:

  • OS X

For all computers:

  • 2 GB of RAM
  • 2 GB of free disk space
  • Broadband Internet connection (DSL or cable recommended)
  • Web browser (Google Chrome is highly recommended)
  • Sound card with speakers or headphones
  • Printer

Browser Requirements

  • Google Chrome works best with BlackBoard. Other browsers such as Internet Explorer or Safari may be used, but may not support all functionalities in BlackBoard, such as online quizzes and the HTML editor.
  • All browsers should have JavaScript, Cookies, and Pop-ups enabled (do not block Pop-ups).
  • JavaScript must be enabled
    https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/gp/howtoscript
  • Cookies must be enabled
    http://windows.microsoft.com/en-us/windows-vista/block-or-allow-cookies
  • Pop-up blocker is turned off
    https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/kb/909604
